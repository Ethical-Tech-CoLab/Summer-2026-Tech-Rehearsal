ABOUT THE ETHICAL TECH COLAB
Opening remarks from the Founder & Lab Director of the Ethical Tech CoLab
WHAT THE LAB DOES
  • Applied research studio housed at NYU SPS Center for Global Affairs
  • Incubates in-house projects at the intersection of emerging tech and ethics
  • Partners with adjacent labs and initiatives across the university
  • Trains graduate researchers through cohort-based practice
ABOUT THE TECH SHOWCASE
A lightning talk showcase where Ethical Tech CoLab cohort members present their semester research on the intersection of emerging technology and human society. Each presenter has 10 minutes to share findings and provoke discussion.
COLAB MISSION
  • How recurring issues like privacy, bias, and autonomy evolve with new technology
  • The idea that ethical challenges are reshaped, not erased, by AI
  • The need for updated governance structures and conceptual tools
